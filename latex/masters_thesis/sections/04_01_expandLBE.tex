\eqref{eq: lattice boltzmann equation} can be reformulated as
\begin{equation}
  \label{eq: lattice boltzmann equation shifted}
  f_{ij}(t + \Delta t, x, y) = f^*_{ij}(t,x - ic \Delta t , y - cj\Delta t), \quad\forall i,j\in \{-1, 0, 1\}
\end{equation}
by shifting $x$ and $y$
