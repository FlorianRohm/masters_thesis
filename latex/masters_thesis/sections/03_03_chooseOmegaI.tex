In the derivation of the Navier-Stokes equations in Section~\ref{sec: Momentum equation} and following, the only parameter which will be needed is $\omega_1$.
Like in \gls{srt}, it will play the role of the viscosity, respectively Reynolds number.
Thus, it's safe to set the other parameters to one according to our second order analysis.

With the speed of sound for the D2Q9 calculated as $c_s^2=\frac{c^2}{3}$, c.f.~\cite[page 175]{wolf2000lattice}, we get
\begin{equation}
  \label{eq:collision equation system}
  \begin{aligned}
    \kappa_{00}^{*} & = \kappa_{00} \\
    \kappa_{10}^{*} & = \kappa_{10} \\
    \kappa_{01}^{*} & = \kappa_{01} \\
    \kappa_{11}^{*} & = (1-\omega_1)\kappa_{11} \\
    \kappa_{20}^{*} - \kappa_{02}^{*}
      & = (1-\omega_1) (\kappa_{20} - \kappa_{02}) \\
    \kappa_{20}^{*} + \kappa_{02}^{*}
      & =  \frac{2c^2}{3} \\
    \kappa_{21}^{*} & = 0 \\
    \kappa_{12}^{*} & = 0 \\
    \kappa_{22}^{*} & = 0
  \end{aligned}
\end{equation}
or equivalently
\begin{equation}
  \begin{aligned}
    \kappa_{00}^{*} & = \kappa_{00} \\
    \kappa_{10}^{*} & = \kappa_{10} \\
    \kappa_{01}^{*} & = \kappa_{01} \\
    \kappa_{11}^{*} & = (1-\omega_1)\kappa_{11} \\
    \kappa_{20}^{*} & = \frac{c^2}{3} + \frac{1}{2}(1-\omega_1) (\kappa_{20} - \kappa_{02}) \\
    \kappa_{02}^{*} & = \frac{c^2}{3} - \frac{1}{2}(1-\omega_1) (\kappa_{20} - \kappa_{02}) \\
    \kappa_{21}^{*} & = 0 \\
    \kappa_{12}^{*} & = 0 \\
    \kappa_{22}^{*} & = 0.
  \end{aligned}
\end{equation}

As we want to compute with the normalized cumulants,~\eqref{eq: definition normalized cumulants}, we need to multiply each line with $m_{00}$.
Hence, the final collision reads
\begin{equation}
  \label{eq: final collision one relaxation}
  \begin{aligned}
    K_{00}^{*} & = K_{00} \\
    K_{10}^{*} & = K_{10} \\
    K_{01}^{*} & = K_{01} \\
    K_{11}^{*} & = (1-\omega_1)K_{11} \\
    K_{20}^{*} & = \frac{m_{00}c^2}{3} + \frac{1}{2}(1-\omega_1) (K_{20} - K_{02}) \\
    K_{02}^{*} & = \frac{m_{00}c^2}{3} - \frac{1}{2}(1-\omega_1) (K_{20} - K_{02}) \\
    K_{21}^{*} & = 0 \\
    K_{12}^{*} & = 0 \\
    K_{22}^{*} & = 0.
  \end{aligned}
\end{equation}
